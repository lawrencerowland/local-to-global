Portfolio Wave · Foray 140

Local to Global

A series of deliberately separate essays about a stubborn project question: when can many locally sensible accounts be patched into one coherent global view—and when is their failure to patch the most useful result?

Can scope, state, schedule or risk be represented so that a project-wide view is composed from local data without erasing the disagreements at its boundaries?The main foray question

Local firstKeep each team’s own useful view before attempting composition.
Overlaps explicitSay which facts must agree across which interfaces.
Failure is evidenceA mismatch can locate an obstruction rather than merely create a red light.
Checker, not oracleLet language models propose structure; let declared rules judge compatibility.
Plural essaysHold several concrete attempts apart until later integration is earned.
